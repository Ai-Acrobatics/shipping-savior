## Executive Summary

Shipping Savior is a vertically integrated logistics intelligence platform combining **freight brokerage commissions**, **SaaS subscriptions**, **FTZ consulting**, and **data intelligence** revenue streams. The platform is purpose-built for international freight operators, importers, and supply chain teams who handle cold chain, general cargo, and SE Asia consumer goods flows.

### Key Financial Highlights (18-Month)

| Metric | Conservative | Base | Optimistic |
|--------|-------------|------|------------|
| Total Revenue (M18) | $87,400/mo | $164,200/mo | $298,500/mo |
| Gross Margin at M18 | 62% | 71% | 78% |
| Break-Even Month | M14 | M11 | M8 |
| Required Funding | $480,000 | $380,000 | $280,000 |
| Cumulative Revenue (18 mo) | $621,000 | $1,094,000 | $1,847,000 |
| ARR at M18 | $1,049,000 | $1,970,000 | $3,582,000 |

### Four Revenue Pillars

1. **Freight Brokerage Commissions** — 2–5% of shipment GMV; founder's existing core competency
2. **SaaS Platform Subscriptions** — $149/mo to $1,499/mo; calculator suite, dashboards, route intelligence
3. **FTZ Consulting Retainers** — $3,000–$12,000/mo; duty optimization strategy, incremental withdrawal planning
4. **Data Intelligence Subscriptions** — $299–$999/mo; tariff alerts, market intelligence, competitor benchmarking

### Why This Model Works

The founder already has **an operating freight brokerage business** with real shipment GMV. Phase 1 technology investment ($180K) directly augments existing revenue by systematizing manual workflows. SaaS subscriptions leverage the same calculators already built for the proposal site. FTZ consulting is a premium extension of a proven operational capability. The model doesn't require a cold start — it wraps software around an existing revenue-generating operation.

```

────────────────────────────────────────    ──────────────────────────────────────

FTZ CONSULTING (Premium)                    DATA INTELLIGENCE (Recurring)
────────────────────────────────────────    ──────────────────────────────────────
• Status election strategy (irrevocable)    • Tariff change alerts (HTS updates)
• Incremental withdrawal modeling           • SE Asia market intelligence
• Duty rate locking strategy                • Shipping rate benchmarks
• April 2025 exec order compliance          • Competitor pricing data feeds
• Retainer: $3,000–$12,000/mo             • Subscription: $299–$999/mo
```

### Revenue Architecture Rationale

- **Brokerage first**: Zero incremental cost — founder already performs this work manually. Tech systematizes and scales it.
- **SaaS second**: Monetizes the calculators and intelligence tools built for the proposal site; very low marginal cost per user.
- **Consulting third**: High-margin ($200+/hr effective rate) premium layer for clients with complex FTZ/duty situations.
- **Data fourth**: Recurring passive revenue once tariff monitoring infrastructure is built; minimal marginal cost.

**Target**: Solo freight brokers, small importers (<$500K annual cargo value)

**Included**:
- Landed cost calculator (unlimited calculations)
- HTS code lookup and duty estimator (10 lookups/day)
- Container utilization calculator
- Basic route comparison (2 routes)
- PDF export (5/month)
- Email support (48h response)
- 1 user seat

**Limits**:
- No FTZ savings analyzer
- No API access
- No custom reporting
- No backhaul intelligence

**Unit Economics**:
- Gross margin: ~82% (mostly infrastructure + support)
- Support cost: $8/mo
- Infrastructure allocation: $3.50/user/mo
- Net contribution: $137.50/mo

### Incremental Withdrawal Modeling

FTZ allows paying duty only on goods **as they leave** the bonded warehouse — not all at once.

**Cash Flow Advantage of Incremental Withdrawal**:

| Metric | Standard Import | FTZ Incremental |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| Duty payment timing | Day of entry (lump sum) | As sold (distributed) |
| Duty on 50,000 units | $1,325 paid upfront | $26.50 per 1,000-unit pallet |
| Working capital tied up in duty | $1,325 | $0 at entry |
| Float benefit (6% cost of capital) | — | $79.50/container |
| Flexibility if goods don't sell | None (duty already paid) | Only pay duty on units sold |

**Unsold inventory protection**: If 20% of inventory is returned to origin or destroyed, importer avoids duty on that 20% entirely ($265 saved per container).
